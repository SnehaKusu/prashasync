PRASHA SAFETY

health safety

With the rise of large language models (LLMs) in mental health apps, user safety is more crucial than ever. As pioneers in AI mental healthcare, Prasha Sync created a safety benchmark to evaluate LLMs across real-world mental health scenarios.

The benchmark uses expert-reviewed prompts to assess model responses for safety, scoring each scenario individually and averaging them for an overall mental health safety score.
